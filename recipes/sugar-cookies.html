Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/8 teaspoon fine salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and line a small baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Stir to combine the melted butter, sugar, egg yolk, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt evenly over the dough and stir just to combine
  4. Press dough flat and evenly in the bowl and then divide in half. You should get 3 cookies from each half (6 total)
  5. Roll each dough ball lightly in granulated sugar and space evenly on the baking sheet
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until they spread, start to crackle, and appear dry on top
  7. Let the cookies rest on the baking sheet for 2 minutes before moving them to a cooling rack
